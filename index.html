Research
September 2021 – Present

Harvard University
Edge Computing Lab

Visiting Undergraduate Researcher
  • Enabling a constrained quadruped robot platform to overcome cheap hardware through intelligence
  • Modeled a reinforcement learning problem of a toy robot using OpenAI Gym & PyBullet to achieve walking gaits
  • Applied an imitation learning framework to achieve robust natural-looking walking gaits
Summer 2020

Cornell University
Summer Program for Undergraduate Research (SPUR)

Mathematics Undergraduate Researcher
  • Applied optimal control theory to study bifurcations in the construction of elastic Möbius bands
  • Computed the critical twist angle of a rod with an infinite bending stiffness for various twisting stiffnesses
  • Discovered the emergence of a supercritical pitchfork bifurcation at the points of instability
  • Discovered that all branches emerging from bifurcations occurring before 180° lead to the same Möbius shape
  • Created animations using MATLAB to communicate my results at the Young Mathematicians Conference

Smart Healthcare Research Assistant

  • Led the development of a recommender system to provide adaptive recommendations to Alzheimer caregivers
